Residential Facility ?

Parul University has established an in-house residential complex with separate provision for boys and girls with a capacity of accommodating 10000+ student. All hostels have 24 hour water (timely hot water in winter), electric supply, WiFi etc. On campus amenities such as Gymnasium, Swimming Pool, Open Air Theatre, Banking Service and Hospital are also available to the students. Separate hostel blocks for NRIs & International students equipped with Air Conditioner & other modern facilities are also available. There are Five Messes on campus which serve nutritious Breakfast, Lunch, High Tea & Dinner to the hostel residents.

Student Career Services ?

The main aim of Parul University is to provide an array of job opportunities to students. The University plays an important role in providing jobs rather than seeking jobs. For this, the University has established various cell/departments to provide support to students in pursuing a career as per their interest.

World Class Infrastructure

Spread over the sprawling 150 acres of beautifully landscaped area, the Parul University campus is amongst the most distinguished ones in India. Massive educational institutions set in lush green environs provide the right ambience and atmosphere for intellectual stimulation. The cutting-edge infrastructure in forms of auditoriums, seminar rooms, class rooms, computer laboratories, library, and Students’ hostels equipped with modern age amenities, canteens serving nourishing & nutritious food, etc facilitate teaching-learning process that fascinate and delight students, faculty, corporate dignitaries, foreigners and parents.

Quality Placement

Parul University is one of the leading institutions across Gujarat which is renowned for its excellent placement record. It’s Training & Placement Cell with dynamic, proactive & experienced Training & Placement Officers work hard in establishing effective industry-institute interface resulting in excellent placement scenario & smooth transition of students from academics to professional life. 1000+corporates visit the University for conducting Campus Interviews every year and over 750 Industry Representatives visit the campus every year to interact with students strengthening the industry-academic relationship. For further strengthening the ties with the industry & incorporating their requirements in the curriculum, HR Summit is organized every year across Gujarat which includes participation of more than 265 Industry Representatives serving as CEOs, MDs, CMDs, Senior HR Managers in the prominent organizations such as ESSAR, Larsen & Toubro, Borosil, JK Papers, SANOFI, Rishabh Infotech, Collabera Technology, Xylem, State Bank of India, CEAT tyres, VODAFONE, GIPCL, GACL, E-Tech etc.
